## Barbecue

The original flavour. Marinated in a blend of barbecue sauce, soy sauce, Worcestershire. A sweet blend with a chewy consistency.

---

## Smokey BBQ

The classic barbecue flavour with a Smokey edge. Dryer than its brother, with a hint of campfire that keeps you coming back.

---

## Teriyaki

Sweet, sticky and full in flavour. A rich glaze of teriyaki sauce and soy for those who love bold Japanese flavours.

---

## Korean BBQ

Bulgogi marinade with a dash of soy and fresh garlic - Korean BBQ with its rich, unique flavour is a must try.

---

## Sweet Chilli

The familiar taste of sweet chilli creates a tacky beef jerky different to the other flavours. A very mild spice that is hardly noticed.

---

## Exotic Spice

A slightly spicy, South Korean marinade gives Exotic Spice its uncanny flavour. Tends to be dryer than other flavours.

---

## Hot Chilli

A Hot Chilli that never lets the chilli fans down. Its barbecue flavour is dried out by chilli and the seeds give it extra texture and heat.

---

## Roast Lamb

Lamb topside is a lean cut of lamb that is perfect for Jerky. A barbecue base with fresh rosemary and garlic - tastes just like a Sunday roast.

---
